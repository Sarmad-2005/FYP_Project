2.4 Functional Requirements
Module 1 – Project Monitoring & Performance
The system shall track project milestones, deliverables, and performance indicators in real time.


The system shall compare planned vs. actual project progress to identify deviations.


The system shall detect operational bottlenecks and visualize workflow inefficiencies.


The system shall provide dashboards with live project health summaries for managers.


Module 2 – Financial & Audit Management
The system shall monitor and record all project-related budgets and expenditures.


The system shall detect anomalies, fraud, or financial irregularities


The system shall analyze effort versus outcomes to assess financial performance.


The system shall generate automated audit summaries for compliance officers.


Module 3 – Risk Forecasting & Simulation
The system shall forecast project delays and risk probabilities using predictive analytics.


The system shall simulate “what-if” scenarios to support data-driven decision-making.


The system shall visualize possible outcomes of risk mitigation strategies.


The system shall generate early-warning alerts for potential schedule or cost overruns.


Module 4 – Resource & Task Optimization
The system shall optimize allocation of human and material resources based on workload.


The system shall intelligently prioritize tasks to ensure timely execution.


The system shall recommend reallocation of resources when bottlenecks are detected.


Module 5 – Compliance & Guidance
The system shall continuously monitor adherence to policies, standards, and regulations.


The system shall detect and report compliance violations automatically.


The system shall allow creation of custom rules for specific organizational policies.


The system shall provide real-time guidance and corrective actions for non-compliance.


Module 6 – Knowledge & Document Intelligence
The system shall analyze and summarize project documents in both English and Urdu.


The system shall generate intelligent, concise versions of lengthy reports.


The system shall cluster similar project themes for cross-project knowledge reuse.


Module 7 – Communication Intelligence
The system shall provide a project-specific AI chat hub for team collaboration.


The system shall detect conflicts or contradictory instructions in team communication.


The system shall summarize discussion threads into action items for managers.



Module 8 – AI Guidance & Assistant
The system shall offer an AI assistant accessible via text


The assistant shall answer user queries about project performance, risks, and finances.


The system shall provide context-aware recommendations and insights.


The system shall generate project similarity indexes for comparative analysis.


Module 9 – Mobile Monitoring & Alerts
The system shall provide real-time alerts and notifications for project status changes.


The system shall visualize project data and summaries on mobile dashboards.
